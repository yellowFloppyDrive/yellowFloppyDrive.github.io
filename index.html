What is yellowFloppyDrive?

yellowFloppyDrive is a passion project. The purpose of this project is get better at coding, get better at learning, get better at note taking, get better at simplifying code, get better at all things programming related. The goal is simple: "Get good at coding, keep it simple and enjoy the journey".

Other than that, this is the place where I will be keeping my thoughts and experiences. If you are on a similar journey, you might find this interesting and it might help you. If you do find that anything here helps you, shoot me a message at yellowFloppyDrive@gmail.com. If you find that you are looking for more answers, try visiting one of my channels. If that also does not help, shoot me a message. I will give my best to help you on your journey.

"The colors are very childish. I don't like it." - Well, this is an opinionated project. I like childish and playful, because it is fun and enjoyable. If you don't like it please move along. There are other people who will, or already do find it interesting.
